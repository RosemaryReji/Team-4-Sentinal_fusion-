# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# Sentinel Fusion

### AI-Powered Digital Intelligence Fusion Platform for Digital Forensics & Investigations

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Product Overview

## Product Name

**Sentinel Fusion**

## Product Vision

Sentinel Fusion is an AI-powered Digital Intelligence Fusion Platform
that enables digital forensic investigators and authorized law
enforcement agencies to rapidly transform fragmented digital evidence
into actionable intelligence.

Instead of manually investigating thousands of images, videos, messages,
metadata, and online activities, Sentinel Fusion automatically
correlates evidence, reconstructs timelines, identifies hidden
relationships, detects synthetic media, and prioritizes high-risk
investigative leads.

The platform acts as an **AI investigative assistant**, while ensuring
that **every investigative decision remains under human oversight**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Problem Statement

Modern investigations involve enormous volumes of digital evidence
including:

-   Images
-   Videos
-   Audio recordings
-   Chat exports
-   Social media profiles
-   Cloud storage
-   Device backups
-   Browser histories
-   Metadata
-   GPS locations

Investigators often spend weeks manually reviewing media, extracting
metadata, building timelines, connecting suspects, identifying
relationships, and detecting manipulated content.

Current tools perform isolated forensic analysis but rarely combine
multiple intelligence sources into one unified investigation platform.

This leads to:

-   Slow investigations
-   Missed evidence
-   Hidden relationships remaining undiscovered
-   Investigator fatigue
-   Delayed victim safeguarding

# 5. Product Goals

1.  Reduce digital evidence review time by **70%**
2.  Automatically correlate evidence across multiple digital sources.
3.  Generate explainable investigative intelligence.
4.  Detect AI-generated and manipulated media.
5.  Improve investigative prioritization through AI risk assessment.
6.  Accelerate case progression while maintaining human oversight.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Non-Goals

Sentinel Fusion will **not**:

-   Replace certified forensic tools
-   Make legal decisions
-   Automate arrests
-   Modify or delete evidence
-   Produce autonomous investigative conclusions

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. User Stories

### Evidence Upload

**As a Digital Forensic Investigator**, I want to upload multiple
evidence types so that I can investigate everything from one platform.

### Metadata Extraction

**As an Investigator**, I want metadata extracted automatically so that
I don't manually inspect every file.

### Source Correlation

**As a Case Officer**, I want related evidence automatically connected
so hidden relationships become visible.

### Timeline Reconstruction

**As an Investigator**, I want events reconstructed chronologically so I
understand exactly what happened.

### Synthetic Media Detection

**As an Investigator**, I want manipulated media flagged so I can verify
evidence authenticity.

### Risk Prioritization

**As a Law Enforcement Analyst**, I want investigations prioritized by
risk level so urgent cases receive immediate attention.

### Report Generation

**As an Investigator**, I want reports automatically generated to reduce
documentation effort.
